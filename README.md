# ⚽ Football Maths

A friendly football (soccer) themed addition game for young children
(around 4 years old) who are learning to add numbers up to **20**.

It was designed for a little football fan who already adds numbers with a
sum of 10 or less, and now wants to grow towards 20.

## How to play

1. Open **`index.html`** in any web browser (phone, tablet, or computer).
2. Pick a difficulty on the start screen:
   - **Easy** – sums up to 10 (the warm-up he already knows)
   - **Medium** – sums up to 15
   - **Big** – sums up to 20
3. Tap **▶ PLAY**.
4. **Count the two groups of footballs**, add them up, and tap the football
   with the correct number.
5. A correct answer **kicks the ball into the goal** 🥅, the crowd cheers,
   and a goal is added to the scoreboard.
6. Score **10 goals** to win a trophy celebration 🏆.

## Why it helps with learning

- **It shows the maths, not just the digits.** Each question displays the
  actual footballs to count (e.g. ⚽⚽⚽ ➕ ⚽⚽), so a pre-reader can solve it
  by counting before recognising the written numbers.
- **Gentle and encouraging.** A wrong tap just gives a little wobble and a
  soft sound — no failing, no losing. He can try again straight away.
- **Multiple choice with close numbers** keeps it achievable while still
  making him think.
- **Big, round, tappable footballs** suit small fingers and touchscreens.

## Good to know

- **No internet, no installation, no accounts.** It is a single HTML file.
  Everything — sounds included — is generated in the browser, so it works
  completely offline.
- **Sound** can be toggled with the 🔊 button in the top-right corner.
- Best played in **landscape** on a tablet or phone, but it adapts to any
  screen.

## Tip for grown-ups

Sit with him for the first few rounds and count the footballs out loud
together ("one, two, three... and four, five — that's *five*!"). Once he
gets the idea, let him take over. When Easy feels too simple, switch to
Medium, then Big.

Have fun, and score lots of goals! ⚽
